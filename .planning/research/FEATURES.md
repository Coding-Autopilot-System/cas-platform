# Feature Research

## Table stakes

- Environment separation and consistent naming/tags
- Managed identity and least-privilege boundaries
- Centralized logs, metrics, and application telemetry
- Repeatable validation and non-deploying what-if
- Cost budgets and ownership metadata
- Architecture, threat model, operations, and contribution policies

## Deferred differentiators

- Private networking and controlled egress
- Federated deployment workflows and approvals
- Azure Policy assignments and compliance reporting
- SLOs, alert routing, dashboards, and automated rollback
- Signed releases, SBOMs, and deployment evidence attestations
